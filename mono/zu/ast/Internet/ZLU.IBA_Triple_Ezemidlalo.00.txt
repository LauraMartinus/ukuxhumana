Ezemidlalo4.8 EZEMIDLALO
 
I-IBA iphakamisa ukuthi: 
  kutholwe icebo elizoxhaseka ngokwezimali ukuthola amalungelo asemqoka 
  ezemidlalo futhi kuhlolwe ukubekwa komthetho ovimbela ukumuncwa lamalungelo 
  (njengakwamanye amazwe athile) mayelana nalokhu. 
  umsakazo kawonkewonke kufanele uzame ukwenza imidlalo ingabandlululi kodwa 
  ihehe kakhulu futhi ihambisane nalokho okuthandwa izethameli okuyizona 
  eziningi.
