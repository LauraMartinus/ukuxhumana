Umhlanganyeli omusha we-Protea  

Ukwakheka kwamahotela eProtea
Amahotela eProtea abunjwa ngo 1984 July 1, ngesisekelo sokuqala sezakhiwo eziwu-4 ezaziphethwe uStehlik nabahlanganyeli bakhe, amahotela iHeerengracht neCapetonian aseKapa, ihotela iProtea Gardens eseGoli neSan Lameer Resort eseNatal South Coast.  Inkampani yayiphethwe iBankorp ngamapesenti angu-60 noStehlik nabahlanganyeli bakhe ngo 40%, ababeyiziphathimandla zenkampani.

Ukukhula kwawo
Uhlelo lokwandiswa olusheshayo lwalandela isibalo samahotela ngaphansi kweProtea sanyuka ngo-20 ekupheleni kwa 1984 sakhuphuka ngo-40 ngo 1987.  Zinikeza indawo yokuhlala esiluhlobo lwenkanyezi enokukhanya kwengilazi enezinhlangothi ezintathu, ezine nezinhlanu.  Amahotela ayesewa,ayeshintshwa afakwe esimweni esinenzuzo ezimalini.


Ukuthenga kwabaphathi
Ngo 1987 oStehlik bathenga amasheya kaBankorp ekuphatheni nezakhiwo zenkampani.  UStehlik nezinye iziphathimandla anazo ezingu-6 bese bephatha iProtea ngokugcwele 100%.  Ngo 1994 amanye amalunga angu-5 akhethwa ukuphatha amahotela eProtea.

Ukwakhiwa kabusha
Ngo 1988 July 1, inkampani ephethe ezezimali, iProtea Hospitality Corporation (PTY) Limited, yaqala ukusebenza NGEMIKHAKHA emithathu:
Amahotela eProtea
Isu lokuqala
Izakhiwo zeProtea

Ukubambisana noFedics
Ngo July 1990 ngenxa yezizathu zenzuzo edalwa ukuhlanganyela iqembu leFedics (inkampani enkulu kunazo zonke e-Africa ukuthengisa ngokudla) yazuza u 40% wengxenye yeProtea.  Lokhu kunikeza wonke amahotela eProtea intuba yokungena kakhudlwana elwazini olunobuhlakani bakaFedics kanye nasekutholeni isisekelo kubaphathi bayo (barlows, Sanlam, ICS, Servgro) 

Umhlanganyeli omusha weProtea
Njengenxenye enkulu yabo ukusebenza ngokubambisana noProtea, uFedics usenqume ukuphuca uProtea amasheya.  Umhlanganyeli wethu omusha manje iCommonwealth Development Corporation (CDC)
ICDC iphethwe uhulumeni waseBritain kanti umthetho sivivinyo okwamanje usaphambi kwephalamende laseBritain ozovumela iCDC ishintshelwe ekuhlanganyeleni kwabafinile bodwa nokwabantu bonke, wavumele ukwethulwa kwezimali zangasese.
ICDC ngumtshali zimali okhuthele ezinhlelweni zokushintshwa komnotho.  Iyatshala izimali, iphathe futhi ilekelele ukusebenziswa kwamabhizinisi asebenza kahle ekwakheni imali lawo aphethwe abantu abathile.  Ekupheleni konyaka ka 1998, iCDC yatshala amadola angu-2.5billion emabhizinisini angu-400 emazweni angu-55.

Ukwakheka kwamahotela eProtea
Amahotela eProtea abunjwa ngo 1984 July 1, ngesisekelo sokuqala sezakhiwo eziwu-4 ezaziphethwe uStehlik nabahlanganyeli bakhe, amahotela iHeerengracht neCapetonian eseKapa, ihotela iProtea Gardens eseGoli neSan Lameer Resort eseNatal South Coast.  Inkampani yayiphethwe iBankorp ngamapesenti angu-60 noStehlik nabahlanganyeli bakhe ngo 40%, ababeyiziphathimandla zenkampani.

Ukukhula kwawo
Uhlelo lokwandiswa olusheshayo lwalandela isibalo samahotela ngaphansi kweProtea sanyuka ngo-20 ekupheleni kwa 1984 sakhuphuka ngo-40 ngo 1987.  Zinikeza indawo yokuhlala esiluhlobo lwenkanyezi enokukhanya kwengilazi enezinhlangothi ezintathu, ezine nezinhlanu.  Amahotela ayesewa,ayeshintshwa afakwe esimweni esinenzuzo ezimalini.


Ukuthenga kwabaphathi
Ngo 1987 oStehlik bathenga amasheya kaBankorp ekuphatheni nezakhiwo zenkampani.  UStehlik nezinye iziphathimandla anazo ezingu-6 bese bephatha iProtea ngokugcwele 100%.  Ngo 1994 amanye amalunga angu-5 akhethwa ukuphatha amahotela eProtea.

Ukwakhiwa kabusha
Ngo 1988 July 1, inkampani ephethe ezezimali, iProtea Hospitality Corporation (PTY) Limited, yaqala ukusebenza NGEMIKHAKHA emithathu:
Amahotela eProtea

Ukubambisana noFedics
Ngo July 1990 ngenxa yezizathu zenzuzo edalwa ukuhlanganyela iqembu leFedics (inkampani enkulu kunazo zonke e-Africa ukuthengisa ngokudla) yazuza u 40% wengxenye yeProtea.  Lokhu kunikeza wonke amahotela eProtea intuba yokungena kakhudlwana elwazini olunobuhlakani bakaFedics kanye wasekutholeni isisekelo kubaphathi bayo (barlows, Sanlam, ICS, Servgro) 


Umhlanganyeli omusha weProtea
Njengenxenye enkulu yabo ukusebenza ngokubambisana noProtea, uFedics usenqume ukuphuca uProtea amasheya.  Umhlanganyeli wethu omusha manje iCommonwealth Development Corporation (CDC)
ICDC iphethwe uhulumeni waseBritain kanti umthetho sivivinyo okwamanje usaphambi kwephalamende laseBritain ozovumela iCDC ishintshelwe ekuhlanganyeleni kwabafinile bodwa nokwabantu bonke, wuvumele ukwethulwa kwezimali zangasese.
ICDC ngumtshali zimali okhuthele ezinhlelweni zokushintshwa komnotho.  Iyatshalwa izimali, iphathe futhi ilekelele ukusebenzisa kwamabhizinisi asebenza kahle ekwakheni imali labo aphethwe abantu abathile.  Ekupheleni konyaka ka 1998, iCDC yatshala amadola angu-2.5billion emabhizinisini angu-400 emazweni angu-55.

Ukubona okunye okuningi ungaphakathi endaweni kukhona uhambo lwesitimela I-Outeniqua choo choo esihamba phakathi kwe-Knysna neGeorge, nohambo lwezinyawo ukuya eTsitsikamma National Park kanye nakwedumile I-Otter Trail.

October 1999 

Amahotela aseProtea ayaziqhenya ukumemezela ukuqala kokusebenza kwentsha I-Premier Protea Hotel - iProtea Hotel Wanderers.  Itholakala ezinkundleni ezilondekile zekilabhu I-Wanderers, umklamo odle u-R100 million, emaphethelweni ezindawo ezihlala abantu emadolobheni ezidumile nezisemazingeni aphezulu eGoli, I-Illovo neSandton, lihlelwe ukuthatha amehlo abantu uma ivula iminyango uvulwa ngokokuqala enyangeni kaOctober kulonyaka.

"Siyaziqhenya ngokukwazi ukuveza amahotela angaphezu kuka-100 eqembu leProtea, asebenza emazweni awu-10 ase-Afrika nasemazweni aseziqhingini ze-Indian Ocean.  Sijabula kakhulu, kakhulukazi ngeProtea Hotel Wanderers, ngobunjalo bayo eyahlula wonke ngesikulindele" kusho u-Arthur Gillis, iManaging Director weProtea Hotels.

IProtea ikholelwa ekuthini ngenxa yegama labo elivele lisebenza, ukusebenza kwabo neqembu lomsebenzi nomsebenzi wabo odlule kwezinje izimakethe ezifana nabo, banesiqiniseko sokuphumelela ngeProtea Hotel Wanderers ngaphandle kwamanye amahotela akhona eGauteng.  Ihotela lenziwa laba sendaweni enhle ekusebenzeni nabantu bezwe nezivakashi zamazwengamazwe kungasabalwa ukuzijabulisa kwakhona okunhlobonhlobo nezemidlalo. 

Ihotela linamagumbi okulala awu-231 asezingeni, awesimanje ngokuhlotshiswa nokutholakala kuwo.  Ahlelwe kusuka kwamancane ajwayelekile kuya kwamakhulu anabo bonke ubukhazikhazi.  Izivakashi zasehotela kuzozencekelela ukuthi zibe amalunga ahlonishwayo ekilabhu iWanderers, enikeza ezinye izinto ngaphandle kwezasehotela, njengezindawo lapho kubanjelwa khona imihlangano nezindawo zokuthengisa ngokudla, nokuningi kwezemidlalo nezokuzithokozisa.  Kulezo zinto kukhona kuzo ukufundela ukudlala igalofu lohlobo lwe-18 hole, inkundla yethenisi, inkundla yebowls, idamu lokubhukuda nesquash court.

IProtea Hotel Wanderers yakhiwe ngenhlanganisela yemibono phakathi kwe-UK based Morrison Construction Group PLC, iIndustrial Development Corporation (IDC) of SA Ltd., Ebuhlanti Investment (Pty) Ltd. kanye neqembu elikhulu lase-Afrika lamahotela aseProtea.  IMorrison iphethe amaphesenti awu-50 ukulinganisa futhi abaphathi bemklamo, bese kuthi lawa asele kube awabo bonke abanye abahlanganyeli.  Amahotela aseProtea ayethole inkontileka yemiklamo ka 100m ngoSeptember idlule, ngaphezu kwenani lamagama amazwe ngamazwe.
